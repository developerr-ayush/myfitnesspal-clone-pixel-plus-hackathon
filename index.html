Q&A

How do I track calories and nutrition with MyFitnessPal?

Simply search for the food you're eating in our extensive database, or scan a barcode to instantly log it. You can also create custom foods and recipes for items you eat regularly.

What nutrition data can I track besides calories?

MyFitnessPal tracks macronutrients (carbs, protein, fat), micronutrients (vitamins and minerals), fiber, sugar, cholesterol, sodium, and more to give you a complete picture of your nutrition.

How does MyFitnessPal work with other fitness trackers?

MyFitnessPal connects with over 50 apps and devices, including Fitbit, Garmin, Apple Health, and more. This allows you to automatically sync your exercise, steps, and weight data.

Can I track meals, workouts, water, or macronutrient intake?

Yes! MyFitnessPal allows you to track all of these metrics. You can log meals, record workouts, track water intake, and monitor your macronutrient distribution to ensure you're meeting your specific goals.
